## Why start here

The point of Sonic Business is not to keep up with every platform at once. It is to keep a readable home for releases, show notes, and the bits of writing that usually vanish into timelines.

## What this archive should hold

- release notes with enough detail to be useful later
- short show reports and lineup context
- newsletter-style posts that connect the dots between projects

## Next

The immediate goal is simple: publish consistently enough that the archive becomes worth revisiting.
